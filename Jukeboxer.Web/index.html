Hosting a dance party? Use Jukeboxer to help manage the playlist and special requests.

Everyone can participate

The setlist for your party is accessible at a custom jukebxr.com/yourparty address. Anyone with an internet-enabled phone or device can vote!

Each vote counts

Every vote has the potential to change what plays next. Jukeboxer automatically reorders the playlist based on the number of requests.

Load an existing playlist or build your own from songs in your library.
