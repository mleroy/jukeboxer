Privacy policy

Jukeboxer uses your Internet connection to broadcast the following information:

  • Name of your party
  • Songs from your Music library
  • Currently playing song

Jukeboxer does not transmit personal data that could be used to identify you personally.

Transmitted data (name of the party, songs) is stored on Jukeboxer's server only for the duration of your party. It is not persisted after you close the application.
